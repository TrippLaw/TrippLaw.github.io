Family History

Lance and Janet Flower met at Bob Jones University in 1962. Lance was a piano major; Janet, chemistry. They married in 1965 and had two daughters, Donna and Laura.


Lance is an immensely creative and talented keyboard artist. He enjoys sharing his arrangements with others, whether it is playing in church, assisted living homes, or concert halls.


Janet is an avid reader, a love she shares with her daughters. All of them have used the years of enjoying good literature to write and share works of their own. 

How Sweet The Sound

An 80th Birthday Concert by Lance Flower

Shoshana's Refuge

A Novel by Donna Flower Lawrence

During the reign of Solomon, traditions, expectations, and the laws of the God of Jacob rise up like a prison around Shoshana, but she takes refuge in her favorite places: her kitchen and the top of the hill where she feels the breeze in her hair. When an accident forces her to take refuge under the law of God and away from all that she loves, she struggles to see God’s grace in her circumstances. Will she find freedom among God’s people? Or will she seek refuge somewhere else?

Lineage of Grace

A Christmas drama by Laura Flower Hotchkiss, Janet Flower, and Donna Flower Lawrence

Music interspersed with monologues testifying of God's grace to the five women mentioned in the family tree of Christ detailed in Matthew 1. Based on the novellas by Francina Arnold.
